About Me

Fullstack software engineer with focus on frontend, a background in finance, and a passion for crypto

I grew up creative and free-spirited, always making something whether it was out of clay, paint, textiles, or music. At some point, I tried very hard to "grow out" of that, but I love that in coding, I've found a way to reconnect with my childhood and I can once again, create beautiful things.

My exposure to tech was quite minimal until early adulthood. In highschool another student shared a teaching demo on some basic HTML and CSS and although I was immediatly intrigued, I didn't have the confidence or support to explore that interest. What it actually took, was a personal misson and maybe a small existential crisis. Ten years and a finance career later, I quit my job, and completed a highly selective coding bootcamp.

Ultimatly, what inspired me to make a career change; I didn't feel fullfilled in my work, meanwhile I became obsessed with crypto and how this technology could change peoples lives. I fell in love with the idea of a more open and inclusive financial system The space is still nascent, but I wanted to at some point, in some way, be involved in this rare opportunity to make a positive change for the world.
